Welcome to Keep The Beat!

Controls: Left Key = Kick - - Right Key = Snare - - Spacebar = Pause

This game is designed to see how closely you can recplicate and keep to a simple rhythm, and to practice and improve your consistency and accuracy. Once you select 'Begin Game', a rhythm will play continuously and you have to copy it as accurately as possible. Rhythms will consist of two possible elements, the kick drum and the snare drum, and repeat every 4 beats (one bar). Use the left and right arrow buttons to play them in time. You'll be given a score that updates in real time.

You can select one of four simple rhythms, and adjust the tempo to change difficulty.

Keep practicing to drive your millisecond accuracy down, and download your scorecard to keep track of progress.
